# Overview

Welcome to the 11th Competition on Legal Information Extraction and Entailment (COLIEE-2024). This competition is run in association with the [International Workshop on Juris-Informatics (JURISIN 2024)](http://research.nii.ac.jp/~ksatoh/jurisin2024/).

The COLIEE-2024 workshop will be held during JURISIN 2024 from May 28-31, 2024, at the [ACT CITY Hamamatsu, Hamamatsu, Shizuoka, Japan](https://www.actcity.jp/english/).
